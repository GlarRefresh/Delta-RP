1. Community Conduct

  • 1.1 Respect: Treat everyone with kindness. No harassment.
  • 1.2 Discrimination: Zero tolerance for hate speech.
  • 1.3 Privacy: No doxxing or sharing real-life info.
  • 1.4 Toxicity: Do not start or fuel drama.
  • 1.5 Language: Use English for moderation.

2. Chat & Media

  • 2.1 Spamming: No flooding chat/emojis.
  • 2.2 Pinging: Do not ping staff without reason.
  • 2.3 NSFW: No adult content or gore.
  • 2.4 Advertising: No outside links in DMs/channels.
  • 2.5 Spoiler Tags: Use `||spoiler||` for secrets.

3. Roleplay Mechanics

  • 3.1 Godmoding: No invincible characters.
  • 3.2 Powerplaying: Don't control others' characters.
  • 3.3 Metagaming: No using OOC info in-game.
  • 3.4 Literacy: Use at least 2-3 sentences.
  • 3.5 OOC Markers: Use `(( ))` or `//` for OOC.

4. Characters & Lore

  • 4.1 Approval: Staff must approve characters first.
  • 4.2 Canon Slots: One Canon character per person.
  • 4.3 OC Design: Must fit the Deltarune universe.
  • 4.4 Crossovers: No characters from other franchises.
  • 4.5 Continuity: Stick to the server timeline.

5. Staff & Enforcement

  • 5.1 Staff Word: The Knight and Kris have the final say.
  • 5.2 Loophole Abuse: Don't try to bypass rules.
  • 5.3 Respectful Appeals: Appeal bans calmly in DMs.
  • 5.4 Reporting: Use tickets to report rule breakers.
  • 5.5 Common Sense: If it seems wrong, it probably is.
